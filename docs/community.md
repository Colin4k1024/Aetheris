# Aetheris Community

Join our community to connect with other developers, get help, and share your projects.

---

## Community Platforms

### Discord (Recommended)

Join our Discord server for real-time discussions:

**[Join Discord](https://discord.gg/PrrK2Mua)**

#### Channel Structure

| Channel | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `#announcements` | Product updates, releases, news |
| `#general` | General discussion |
| `#introductions` | Introduce yourself |
| `#help` | Get help with Aetheris |
| `#show-and-tell` | Share your projects |
| `#feedback` | Feature requests, suggestions |
| `#jobs` | Job opportunities |

#### Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `@admin` | Project maintainers |
| `@contributor` | Active contributors |
| `@user` | General members |

## Community Guidelines

1. **Be Respectful** - Treat everyone with respect
2. **Stay on Topic** - Keep discussions relevant
3. **No Spam** - No promotional content without permission
4. **Help Others** - Share your knowledge
5. **Follow Code of Conduct** - See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
